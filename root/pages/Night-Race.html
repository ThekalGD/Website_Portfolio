NIGHT RACE

ARCADE 3D RACING GAME

PITCH

Race down an infinite, procedurally generated highway, dodging obstacles as the camera closes in from behind. The further you go, the faster the pace becomes if the camera overtakes you, it’s over. Survive as long as possible, collect coins along the road, and pull off aerial tricks to maximize your score.

Itch page

CONTEXT

Night Race is a student project developed over the course of one month in Unreal Engine 5. Our team consisted of ten members: seven 3D artists and three game designers. To maximize efficiency, the design team specialized based on our individual strengths. I served as the Technical Designer, also responsible for gameplay programming and the 3C’s. My teammates handled documentation, system design, and balancing, while the third focused on project management and cross-department communication with the art team.

> Thekal Lucas - Game Designer_
