FORWARD VECTOR

FIRST-PERSON 3D PLATFORMER

PITCH

Forward Vector is a 3D platformer auto-runner die-and-retry in first-person. Parkour unique levels filled with traps and obstacles, featuring their own abstract futuristic environment and intense electronic track. To complete a level, you’ll have to navigate the entire course as you jump, strafe, slide, and master your air control without taking any damage. As you complete levels, the game becomes increasingly difficult, gradually introducing new obstacles and mechanics.
