INCOLLECTIBLE

2D TWIN STICK SHOOTER

DESCRIPTION

Winner of the b3agz Jam 2025, InCollectible is a twin-stick shooter developed in just one week. Created for the theme "That's not a Collectible", our team of four consisted of a pixel artist who also handled the narration, two sound designers/composers, and myself as the Lead Game Designer and Developer. We interpreted the theme with a dark, ironic twist: "We’re not just collectibles." Instead of playing as the hero, you step into the role of a sentient coin seeking bloody revenge on the greedy adventurers who try to "collect" you.
